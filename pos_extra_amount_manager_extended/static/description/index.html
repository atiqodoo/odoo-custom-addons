POS Extra Amount & Base Profit Manager

Complete Two-Tier Commission System with Full Accounting Integration

Professional two-tier commission distribution for Point of Sale orders with automatic journal entries following Anglo-Saxon accounting principles.

Two-Tier Commission System

🎯 TIER 1: Extra Amount

Track premium pricing above pricelist

  • Calculate: (Paid - Pricelist) × Quantity
  • Quantity toggle per line
  • 3 journal entries per distribution
  • Must complete 100% before TIER 2

💎 TIER 2: Base Profit

Share profit on pricelist itself

  • Calculate: (Pricelist excl VAT - Cost) × Qty
  • Same quantity toggle applies
  • 1 simple journal entry
  • Unlocked after TIER 1 complete

Complete Workflow

1. Calculate Extra

Track premium pricing

2. Distribute Extra

Must complete 100%

3. Calculate Base

🔓 Unlocked!

4. Share Profit

Distribute margins
