POS Extra Amount Manager

Track and Distribute Extra Charges with Full COGS Accounting

Professional management of extra amounts charged above pricelist prices in Point of Sale orders, with automatic journal entries following Anglo-Saxon accounting principles.

Key Features

📊 Precise Calculation

Automatically calculates extra amounts using: (price_subtotal_incl / qty) - pricelist_price

Works seamlessly with VAT-inclusive pricelists

🔄 Quantity Toggle

Per-line boolean control to calculate extra for:

  • Full quantity (default)
  • Single unit only

💰 Flexible Distribution

Distribute by:

  • Percentage of extra amount
  • Fixed amount

Support for multiple distributions per order

📈 AVCO Cost Tracking

Automatic Cost of Goods Sold calculation using:

  • Average Cost (AVCO) from stock valuation
  • Proportional COGS per distribution

Automatic Journal Entries (Anglo-Saxon)

3 Journal Entries Created per Distribution

1️⃣ Extra Revenue Entry

Account Debit Credit
Cash/M-Pesa Account Distribution Amount -
Extra Revenue Account - Distribution Amount

2️⃣ Product COGS Entry

Account Debit Credit
COGS - Product Account Proportional COGS -
Stock Valuation Account - Proportional COGS

3️⃣ Commission Payout Entry

Account Debit Credit
COGS - Commission Account Distribution Amount -
Cash/M-Pesa Account - Distribution Amount

Easy 4-Step Workflow

1. Calculate

Click "Calculate Extra Amount" button on POS order

2. Toggle

Set quantity calculation per line (optional)

3. Distribute

Enter percent/amount, select recipient

4. Done!

3 journal entries created automatically
